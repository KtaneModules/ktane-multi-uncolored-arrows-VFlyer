On The Subject Of Coloured Arrows

The point is, there is no point.

On the module are 4 directional buttons, and a display screen in the middle.

If the buttons are not unique multi-coloured, you’re looking at a different module.

On the screen is a random arrow colour. Use the screen colour with the screen colour arrow to determine which colour arrow to press below. On every correct press, the screen will show up a different colour and the arrow colours will change. Keep doing that, and the module will be disarmed after 7 consecutive correct presses.

Pressing an incorrect button will register a Strike, and the module will reset itself with new colours, and reset your streak.
