Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Module                                            Gray Arrows

On the Subject of Gray Arrows

Or Grey Arrows if you’re stupid.

On the module are 4 directional buttons, and a display screen in the middle.

If the buttons are not Gray, you're looking at a different module.

Begin in the position within the below table in which the column is the 3rd character of the serial number and the row is the 6th.

When the module activates, an arrow will be shown on the display. Follow the arrow from the display. and press the arrow of the space you end up on. If you reach the edge of the table, wrap back to the opposite edge.
