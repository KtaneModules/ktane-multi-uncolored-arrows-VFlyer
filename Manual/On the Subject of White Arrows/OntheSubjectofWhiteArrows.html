On the Subject of White Arrows

How is anyone supposed to solve this module?

Select any of the arrow buttons seven times to solve the module. After the first selection, the next arrow selection must be addressed in less than two seconds.

Upon a strike, by either selecting an incorrect arrow or not selecting an arrow in time, you must re-input arrows from square one. The module will automatically solve after the third strike from this module.

To determine which arrow to select, look at one of the arrows.

This arrow that you should look at is colored differently than the others.

Some of the LEDs manipulating the arrow will be reduced and the arrow will either appear blue, red, yellow, green, purple, orange, cyan, grey, or there will not be a change at all. Yes, these arrows do glow in the dark.

Use the charts below based on the color of the arrow and its position to determine which arrow you will need to select next.
