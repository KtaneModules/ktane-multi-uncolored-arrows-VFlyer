On the Subject of Flashing Arrows

Pointy point point pointer pointy point.

This module will have four directional buttons rapidly changing colors, and a display in the middle showing a two-digit number. Each arrow will flash three colors, exactly one of which being black.

To solve this module, first calculate a number. Take the first numerical digit of the serial number (if there are no numbers, use the number of port plates) and add it to the displayed number. Modulo your result by 5. If your new number is zero, make it one, then refer to the below table to find out the arrow to refer to in the next step.
